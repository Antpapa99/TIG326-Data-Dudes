About

Welcome to our Job Search and Skills Matching Tool, created by IT students for IT students!

As fellow IT students, we know firsthand the challenges and aspirations that come with pursuing a career in the IT industry. That's why we've developed this app specifically tailored to simplify the job search process and help you, as an IT student, find relevant job opportunities that match your skills and preferences. Whether you're actively searching for internships, entry-level positions, or exploring potential career paths in the vast IT landscape, our app is here to assist you every step of the way.

What You Can Do on This Site

Key features of this app:

  • Choose between two search options: Jobs and Skills
  • Click the "Match Skills" button to instantly identify job positions that align with your technical expertise
  • Gain valuable insights into the IT job market with our Skills Distribution Graph
  • Explore a curated list of job matches tailored specifically to IT students like you
